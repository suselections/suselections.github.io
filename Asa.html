Asa Kohn

Running for SSMU Representative

Expect More from Your Student Government

Hello! My name is Asa Kohn, and I'm running to represent you on the SSMU Legislative Council. I've been fighting bureaucracy as a constituent and on behalf of the Computer Taskforce, in both the university administration and the unions that are supposed to represent us, for most of the two years I've been a student here. I know how to navigate SSMU's governace, and I'm committed to making it easier for you. We need to make both SSMU and the SUS more democratic, make sure we have a proportional say as the second-largest faculty, and make sure the fees imposed by the new food sale policies don't stop clubs and department associations from getting the money they need. I have the experience to represent you and get this done, and I hope to do so next year as your elected representative.

Campaign Material

Unfortunately, the SUS rules ban online campaigning anywhere except Facebook. If you would like to talk to Asa about his platform, please come to the event on March 11th at 5:30 PM in the SUS office, BURN 1B21.

We believe SUS rules should promote communication between candidates and voters, so if elected, Asa will push to change this rule.
