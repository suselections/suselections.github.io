Haoyi Qiu

Running for SUS Senator

Hello Science!
My name is Haoyi Qiu (she/her) and I am running to be your 2020-21 Science Senator.
I have been involved with SUS for my past 3 years at McGill. Currently, I am the SUS Speaker of General Council where I moderate debate, make rulings on procedure and oversee all the motions and operations of Council. Previously, I have also been elected to the position of Science Representative to SSMU. These experiences have immensely equipped me with the functionings of student government. I have also sat under various SUS executive portfolios, such as the Academic Events Committee and Clubs & Services Committee allowing me to gain more insight into effective strategies to advocate for the welfare and interest of science students.

Long Platform

OPEN EDUCATION RESOURCES

As your Senator, I will continue the progress on the Open Educational Resources (OERs) project. This project aims to provide students with free digital, low cost printed course materials - greatly increasing the accessibility of education while relieving students of additional financial burdens. In an age where technology is so readily available, OERs open the doors to legal and flexible solutions to combat the ever increasing prices of textbooks. If elected, I will fight for more data collection on feedback methods, and personally meet with professors and advisors to encourage the use and provision of OERs within the Faculty of Science.
