Andrew Lewis

Running for VP External

About:
Hello everyone! My name is Andrew Lewis and I am running to be your SUS VP External!
I am a third year, U2, Physics and Computer Science student from Vancouver, BC. At McGill I have held positions ranging from First Year Rep. to President and I hope to bring my ideas and experience to the position of VP External.

What Does VP External Do:
“In conjunction with the SUS President, the Vice-President, External represents the Society to outside bodies and individuals. The Vice-President, External is also responsible for organizing sponsorships for the Society.” – From the nomination package.
My role as SUS VP External would be to help keep the Science faculties well connected with outside businesses and organizations. By building connection between the diverse student body and outside organizations and clubs I hope to not only obtain sponsorships for McGill student societies, but exciting new opportunities for students as well.

  • Facebook

Long Platform

SPONSORSHIPS & TRANSPARENCY

I would obtain sponsorships from businesses and organizations, such as Red Bull. Additionally I would make it easy for the McGill student body to see what businesses the SUS receives money from, in order to uphold sustainable and equitable practices. Furthermore, I would actively share the list of sponsors with student councils, allowing councils to reach out to organizations, creating new connections and opportunities for students looking for professional experience and sponsorship.

EXPANDING SPONSORSHIP PACKAGE

The Management Undergraduate Society has a sponsorship portal, and package, allowing interested organizations and companies to easily fund and sponsor events and organizations. As VP External I would expand the package that Bob Chang created, adding departments and clubs to the list of SUS events. This would allow sponsors to see what goes on at McGill and reach out to the student initiatives that interest them, resulting in more funding and exposure for students at McGill.

MORE CLUB RESOURCES

Clubs are an integral part of the student body, I would aid these student led initiatives by putting them in contact with companies and professional organizations outside of McGill. This would provide not only sponsorship opportunities, but a way for McGill students to be greater than the McGill bubble.

AFTER HOURS

After Hours was introduced this year, and thus far it has been a massive success! As VP External I would continue the management of this event with a capable and enthusiastic team of students. Additionally, I would continue to reach out to student councils to host After Hours, letting the student body share its creativity and spirit!

If you read this entire thing, thank you for taking the time, I really appreciate it!

1. President of The MSPS:

As President of the MSPS, McGill Society of Physics Students, I am not only responsible for the day to day activities of the MSPS. I also have experience communicating with organizations within and outside McGill to run exciting and new events for all students, most specifically the interdepartmental “Awkward Committee”. Additionally, as President, I have worked to enshrine equity and diversity initiatives into the fabric of the Physics society, I wish to bring this same passion to the SUS.

2. An Active Member of the Student Body:

As a student, I have attended events run by student societies ranging from hot chocolate- board game night to Science Games. As such I am familiar with a wide range of student experiences at McGill, and can contribute my insight into seeking funding for more equitable and diverse events.
